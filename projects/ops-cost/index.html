Coffee Shop Risk Analysis

Excel · Scenario modeling | 2024

ExcelProcess ImprovementOps

Assessed financial and operational risks for a local coffee shop. Modeled potential losses, mitigation costs, and ROI from preventive measures using historic POS data and scenario forecasting.

Method & Findings

  • Data: POS exports, labor schedules, and inventory counts.
  • Analysis: PivotTables, what-if scenarios, and basic control charts.
  • Actions: schedule tuning, waste reduction, tighter reorder points.
  • Result: ~12% operating cost reduction while maintaining service levels.
