Coffee Shop Risk Analysis

Excel · Scenario modeling | 2024

ExcelProcess ImprovementOps

Assessed financial and operational risks for a local coffee shop. Quantified loss exposure and ROI of mitigations using POS history and scenario forecasting.

Method & Findings

  • Framework: Risk register with Likelihood × Impact scoring; heat-map to prioritize top 6 risks.
  • Key risks identified: peak-hour understaffing, milk/pastry waste, stockouts on fast movers, training defects, POS cash variance, audit/compliance slip.
  • Analysis: PivotTables for hour-of-day labor vs sales, control charts for waste variance, reorder-point what-ifs, and basic ROI for mitigations.
  • Mitigations: shift rebalancing, batch sizing for pastries, par levels with safety stock, refresher training, and cash-handling checks.
  • Outcome: ~12% operating cost reduction while holding service levels; lower variance in waste and fewer stockouts.
