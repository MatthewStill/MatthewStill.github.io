Method & Findings

  • Data: Sales, units, and COGS by product and segment; calendar table for time intelligence.
  • Modeling: DAX measures for Sales, COGS, Profit, and Margin %; segment and product drilldowns; YTD/MTD comparisons.
  • Diagnostics: Outlier scan for negative-profit rows; variance vs prior period; contribution analysis by segment and top SKUs.
  • Key findings: The Enterprise segment shows persistent negative profit driven by a few low-margin products and discounts; other segments remain positive.
  • Actions: Remove or re-price loss-making SKUs in Enterprise, reduce discount depth, and shift marketing to higher-margin products.
