Interactive Tableau work exploring pricing sensitivity, feature preferences, and sentiment for a plant-based ice-cream concept.

Interactive Dashboard

Method & Findings

  • Data prep: Cleaned survey responses, normalized scales, built segments (age/diet), extracted text for themes.
  • Modeling: Calculated fields for intent score, WTP bands, NPS, and attribute importance; parameters for price and flavor bundles.
  • Diagnostics: Cohort comparisons, price-step sensitivity, and contribution of attributes to intent.
  • Key findings: Intent peaks at a mid-tier price; dairy-free cohort over-indexes on texture/protein; two flavors drive most positive intent.
  • Actions: Launch mid-tier, lead with top flavors, message protein/texture, A/B test bundles for dairy-free buyers.
