Retail Discount & Margin Analysis (Tableau)

Tableau · Pricing · Margin | 2025

TableauPricingDemand

Dashboards measuring the relationship between discounts, order activity, and margin, plus weekday demand by category.

Method & Findings

  • Data: Orders with sales, COGS, discount %, product category, and calendar dimensions.
  • Modeling: Calculated fields for Margin, Avg. Discount, and weekday/category aggregates; parameters for date and category filters.
  • Diagnostics: Monthly trend of margin vs discount; scatter of orders vs average discount; stacked bars by weekday and category.
  • Findings: Months with higher average discount align with margin compression; orders rise with discount but gains are not margin-accretive past common levels; weekday demand concentrates mid-week with category mix shifts.
  • Actions: Tighten discount bands, reserve deeper promos for low-margin-safe categories, and align staffing to mid-week peaks.
